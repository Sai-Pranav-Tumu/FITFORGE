# FitForge Account Deletion

Last updated: March 31, 2026

## Delete Your Account In The App

You can permanently delete your FitForge account from inside the app:

`Profile > Delete Account`

For security, FitForge may ask you to confirm your password or verify with Google before the deletion finishes.

## What Gets Deleted

When deletion succeeds, FitForge removes:

- Your Firebase Authentication account
- Your FitForge profile stored in Cloud Firestore
- Your local nutrition meal history on the device
- Your saved diet plan on the device
- Your hydration reminder settings and water-tracking data on the device

## What May Remain

Non-personal files such as the downloaded exercise library may remain on the device because they are not tied to your account identity.

## If You Cannot Access The App

If you cannot sign in to the app, publish this page on your website and instruct users to contact the support email listed on your FitForge website or Google Play listing. Ask them to include the email address used for their FitForge account so you can process the deletion request manually.
