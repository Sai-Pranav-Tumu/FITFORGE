# FitForge Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

## Overview

FitForge collects the minimum data needed to create and run a personalized fitness account. This policy explains what data is stored remotely, what stays on the device, how it is used, and how users can delete their account and data.

## Data We Collect

FitForge may collect and store:

- Email address
- Sign-in provider and FitForge user ID
- Name shown in the app
- Profile and personalization data such as age, gender, height, weight, occupation, sitting time, fitness goal, workout days, workout location, equipment access, dietary preference, joint sensitivity, and other workout preference settings

## Data That Stays On The Device

The following information is stored locally on the device unless you add future sync features:

- Hydration reminder settings and water-tracking history
- Saved diet plans
- Nutrition meal history
- Downloaded exercise library files

Downloaded exercise-library files are content only and are not tied to a user's identity.

## How We Use Data

FitForge uses collected data to:

- Create and maintain the user's account
- Authenticate users with email/password or Google Sign-In
- Save onboarding and profile preferences
- Personalize workouts, nutrition suggestions, and dashboard content
- Schedule optional hydration reminders on the device

## Service Providers

FitForge uses the following service providers to operate the app:

- Firebase Authentication
- Google Sign-In
- Cloud Firestore

These providers process data on behalf of FitForge so core app features can work. FitForge does not sell personal data and does not share personal data with advertisers.

## Notifications

If the user enables hydration reminders, FitForge schedules local notifications on the device. These reminders are approximate and may not fire at an exact minute. Users can turn them off at any time from the app.

## Data Retention And Deletion

Users can delete their account inside the app from:

`Profile > Delete Account`

When account deletion is confirmed inside the app, FitForge:

- Deletes the Firebase Authentication account
- Deletes the FitForge profile stored in Cloud Firestore
- Clears local meal history, saved diet plans, hydration reminder settings, and water-tracking data from the device

Non-personal files such as the downloaded exercise library may remain on the device because they are not tied to the deleted account.

## Contact

For privacy questions or manual deletion requests when the app is not accessible, publish this policy with the support contact shown on your website or Google Play listing.
